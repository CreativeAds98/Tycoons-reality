Residential Properties

At Tycoons Reality, we believe your home is more than just a place to live — it’s where life’s best moments unfold. Our residential properties are thoughtfully located in peaceful yet well-connected neighborhoods across Coimbatore. Whether you seek a modern 2BHK apartment or a lavish villa surrounded by nature, we offer a wide variety of homes tailored to every lifestyle and budget. All our homes are built with high-quality materials, elegant architecture, and future-ready amenities.

Each property is designed to deliver comfort, convenience, and community. Residents enjoy access to landscaped parks, children's play areas, fitness centers, swimming pools, and smart home features. Our gated communities ensure 24/7 security, sustainable living practices, and a strong sense of neighborhood. Proximity to reputed schools, healthcare, and commercial zones ensures that everything you need is just a short drive away.

  • Peaceful and green residential zones
  • Energy-efficient and Vastu-compliant designs
  • Ideal for families, retirees, and first-time buyers
Commercial Properties

Tycoons Reality provides high-value commercial real estate solutions that empower businesses to thrive. Our commercial properties include office complexes, high-street retail outlets, showrooms, business centers, and coworking spaces. Industrial Properties

Our industrial property portfolio is tailored for manufacturers, exporters, logistics companies, and warehousing operators. Located in key industrial corridors and SEZ zones of Coimbatore, these properties are well-connected to major highways, railway lines, and transportation networks. From large plots to pre-built factory sheds, we deliver scalable, compliant, and infrastructure-rich solutions.

Each industrial unit is designed for operational efficiency — with high floor loading capacity, large bay spacing, wide road access, and reliable power and water supply. Our industrial parks support 24/7 operations and comply with safety, environmental, and zoning regulations. Whether you're setting up a new facility or expanding your manufacturing footprint, Tycoons Reality provides the perfect industrial base that enables business continuity and growth.

  • Built-to-suit manufacturing facilities
  • Industrial parks with logistics support
  • Affordable land and factory leasing options
